Delimiter Remover

This application allows the user to remove specific delimiters from their text. Simply paste the text you wish to use into the textbox to the right, and select the delimiter you wish to remove from the dropdown box.

Pressing the delimit button will bring bring up a hidden div on the bottom of the input text area containing the result.
