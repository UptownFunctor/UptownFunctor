Not Hotdog

Upload an image and have a convolutional (pretrained resnet) neural network guess whether the image is that of a hot dog, or not hot dog!

The application only accepts jpeg that are less than 10mb in size. If your image exceeds the size limit, the image will still be uploaded to the server, but you will get in return a warning message instead of a classification. You can use this website to resize your image first.
